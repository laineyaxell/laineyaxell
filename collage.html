Collage:

Course Description: This studio course explores collage as a means for developing content and examining complex cultural and material relationships. Projects and assigned texts outline the history of collage as a dynamic art form with a strong political dimension, as well as critically addressing how it is being used today.

Project #1: Reconstruct a photo of yourself into something new
Project #2: Create a collage of a landscape, portrait and a still life
Project #3: Disruptions, political collages
