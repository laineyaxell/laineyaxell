Artificial Intelligence and Baking

Website design for the Booth School of Business Center for Applied AI

My Role

UX Designer

Creating low-fi and high-fi wireframes

UI Designer

Developing the style guide, color palette and other UI elements

Tools

Figma

The Problem & Solution

The Issue

The website before the redesign lacked a cohesive style, was confusing and unclear to users, and the website's purpose was not communicated effectively to users.

Project Goals

Goals included: educating the user on the site's functionality, implementing a complete prototype and creating a visually appealing website.

Design Process

This project focused on synthesizing the needs of users and stakeholders.

Conducting Research

To learn what the needs of the user of the site and the stakeholders were. Priorities included strong interactive elements, encouragment of the search function and emphasis of key categories of baking.

Design

Creating a high fidelity mockup of the intended site to send to developers.

Storyboarding

Understanding why someone would choose to use this site and how they would do so. Defining what the user would hope to accomplish.
